Welcome to i.ganith

General Rules

  • There will be only 1 round in the event.
  • All questions will be based on Mathematics.
  • You will be given 4 options out of which one will be correct.
  • Every question is mandatory to answer.
  • Time duration of round 1 is 90 minutes.
  • Difficulty of questions will be increase from Q-1 to Q-40.
  • Questions will be unidirectional.

How to Play ?

  • The quiz consists of a series of questions.
  • You must solve every question to move to next.
  • You will be given 4 extra chances across 40 questions.
  • If question answered incorrectly you will lose 1 chance and be moved to the next question.
  • Moving forward in similar fashion, on solving all the questions you will successfully solve the quiz.
  • The one who solves most number of questions will be the winner.
  • In case of tie in number of questions, player who would have solved questions fastest will be the winner.
