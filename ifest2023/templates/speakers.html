Speakers

Safin Hasan

India's Youngest IPS Officer

November 5, 2023

11:00 AM Onwards

Being India's Youngest IPS Officer, Mr. Hasan embodies the spirit of unwavering determination and resilience. Despite facing numerous challenges, including a life-altering accident on the way to his exam and battling health issues, his determination remained unshaken. He not only appeared for the UPSC exam but also excelled, securing the second-highest rank in the interview round. Today, he proudly serves as Deputy Commissioner of Police Traffic(East) in Ahmedabad City, proving that with a strong will, there is always a way.

Jatin Chaudhary

Founder of eChai Ventures

November 4, 2023

11:00 AM Onwards

Recognised leader in the entrepreneurial and social impact space, Jatin Chaudhary is a distinguished speaker in i.Fest. His Global Startup Network eChai is a groundbreaking online social networking platform that is dedicated to helping entrepreneurs discover and connect with like-minded individuals. With a remarkable track record of empowering young entrepreneurs and fostering economic opportunities, Jatin brings a wealth of experience and insight making it an invaluable opportunity for attendees to learn from his expertise.

Shiroy Mehta

CEO of Aava Natural Mineral Water

November 5, 2023

5:00 PM Onwards

Managing Director and CEO, Aava Natural Mineral Water Shiroy Mehta is a visionary figure and trailblazer in the natural mineral water and premium beverages market, serving as the CEO of Aava Natural Mineral Water and co-founding Tea Lab India. His ability in negotiation, business development, and sales management reflects his strategic skill. He pursued B.Tech. in Information and Communication Technology from DA-IICT, standing as an exemplary alumnus of our institution. Subsequently, he completed an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad, one of India's premier institutes. His understanding of technology and business administration shines through in his company's continuous integration of technology solutions. His exceptional industry experience and strategic skills make him an exemplary figure and an influential speaker in the realm of business and leadership.
