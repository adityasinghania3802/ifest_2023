About Us

Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Technology (DA-IICT), known for its research and development, has emerged as the torch bearer among the premier technological institution of Gujarat over the past 16 years. i.Fest is one of the most anticipated and acknowledged technical fest all over Gujarat. It is lauded for its versatile and wide ranging technical, social and non-technical events. i.Fest provides an outstanding platform for students to showcase, inculcate the valuable skill of innovation and simultaneously compete with their technical and non-technical skills and win huge amount of cash prizes. Last year, we witnessed over 8000 participants in this 3-day techno-social events.

Kaleido Kronos

Kaleido Kronos, our captivating theme for this year's i.Fest, is a fusion of innovation and timeless grandeur. Inspired by the Greek mythological figure Kronos, the god of time, and the vibrant kaleidoscope, this theme embodies the dynamic nature of technology as it constantly evolves and shapes our world. Just as a kaleidoscope transforms and reimagines its intricate patterns, our tech fest will explore the ever-changing landscape of technology, offering a multifaceted experience where participants can delve into the past, present, and future of tech. Through captivating events, workshops, and exhibits, Kaleido Kronos promises to be an unforgettable journey through the epochs of innovation, where time and technology converge in a kaleidoscope of possibilities. Join us in unraveling the tapestry of time and technology like never before!
